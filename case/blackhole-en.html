Background

Building a predictable, controllable sales engine

In B2B sales, companies constantly need to find new relevant prospects, reach out to them, follow up, and turn them into real sales conversations. This is what makes client acquisition predictable and controllable — and how companies avoid running out of new opportunities while waiting on unpredictable referrals.

In this campaign, SalesBot automated exactly this process. After just 2 months, the results clearly showed that a well-built LinkedIn outreach system does not only create visibility — it can generate directly measurable business value.

Campaign Setup

Multiple channels, multiple steps, fully automated

The campaign started with 2 profiles. After seeing the first results, more profiles were added to scale. SalesBot reached the target audience through multiple channels and several steps.

01
Find Decision Makers
Identified relevant B2B decision makers on LinkedIn.
02
Build Relationships
Followed, liked posts, sent connection requests, and sent InMails to non-acceptors.
03
Send Outreach Messages
Deployed tested outreach messages and multiple follow-ups.
04
Manage & Prequalify Replies
Handled incoming replies and prequalified interested prospects automatically.
05
Follow Up & Convert
Re-engaged undecided prospects and helped generate real sales conversations.

Messages were continuously A/B tested. The better-performing version achieved a 25% reply rate, and the system optimized further in that direction.

Why It Worked

Three things drove the results

1
Precise Targeting
The system did not target generic databases. It reached relevant B2B decision makers — the right people, not just any people. This ensured every message had a real chance of resonating.
2
Quality Messaging
No generic, aggressive sales messages. Instead, messages were tailored to the industry and supported by proof — making them feel relevant and credible rather than intrusive.
3
AI-Based Lead Management
With this level of incoming interest, it is not enough to generate replies. They need to be handled, organized, and followed up quickly. This is exactly where SalesBot creates the most value — turning interest into a pipeline.
