Background

A bold bet on a completely unknown market

Blackhole is a B2B growth agency with 7+ years of experience helping industrial, IT and B2B services companies generate new clients. With 250+ clients across Europe and North America, they had a proven system — but zero presence in the Middle East.

In Q4 2022, the founders Balazs Bolcskei and Marton Toth decided to make a bold move: open a new office in Dubai in just 2 months, with no connections, no brand awareness and no local experience. The goal was to close at least 4 clients to break even — and come home at a profit.

They exceeded that goal by 50%.

The Challenge

Starting from absolute zero

At the time of the decision, neither Balazs nor Marton had ever visited Dubai. They had no market knowledge, no local contacts, no brand recognition and no understanding of the cultural nuances of the Middle East — while operating in one of the most expensive cities in the world.

The question was simple: how do you generate leads and close deals fast when you are a complete stranger in a new market? The answer was their Agency Lead Generation System, powered by SalesBot.

Strategy

The Agency Lead Generation System

The system they had perfected since 2017 relies on three key pillars working together:

01
Online presence & branding
Updated website, added Dubai as a second HQ, switched LinkedIn location to UAE — to look like locals and build trust instantly.
02
Inbound content strategy
Created UAE-specific blog posts and LinkedIn content to show market understanding and build credibility at scale.
03
Value-based outbound campaigns
Segmented LinkedIn + InMail campaigns tailored by industry, focused on relationship building — not pushy pitches.
Execution

Segmented targeting with SalesBot

They defined a precise ICP: small and medium IT, industrial and B2B services companies in the UAE with 20–500 employees, targeting founders, CEOs, managing directors and sales & marketing leaders. The total database was around 10,000 contacts, built via Apollo and Sales Navigator.

They ran 4 campaigns across both founders' LinkedIn profiles, using SalesBot as their multichannel outreach automation platform. The messaging focused on networking and relationship-building — culturally aligned to the Middle East market — with educational content placed strategically in the sequences.
