Building trust and pipeline in a relationship-driven market

In this campaign, SalesBot automated exactly this process. Over 3 months, the results clearly showed that a well-built LinkedIn outreach system does not only create activity — it can generate concrete business opportunities and new paying clients.

Campaign Process

SalesBot is a full AI sales system — not just a messaging tool

The following steps were used to warm up, connect, and convert decision makers in the Outsourced Finance campaign.

01
View the Lead's Profile
The lead's profile was viewed — creating a human first touchpoint before any outreach.
02
Like a Relevant Post
Before the connection request, the system liked one of the lead's posts to reduce cold-contact friction.
03
Connection Request A/B Testing
Two versions were tested. Version B outperformed A — the campaign optimized automatically.
04
Message Sequence
After connection, a tested sequence launched automatically, refined based on reply rates.
05
Manage Leads & Generate Meetings
198 replies processed into 16 qualified leads and 9 real meetings — automatically.

Both connection requests and message sequences were continuously A/B tested. The system optimized toward better-performing versions automatically — based on real data, not assumptions.

Why It Worked

Three things drove the results

1
Warm, Human-Paced Outreach
Profile views and post likes preceded every connection request — making each touchpoint feel natural. Especially important in a relationship-driven market like Hungarian SME financial services.
2
Precise Targeting & Quality Messaging
The campaign reached relevant Hungarian SME decision makers — not generic databases. Messages were tailored to the financial services context, feeling credible rather than intrusive.
3
AI-Based Lead Management
With 198 incoming replies, manual handling would have been a bottleneck. SalesBot organized, tagged, and prequalified leads automatically — so the team could focus on real sales conversations.
